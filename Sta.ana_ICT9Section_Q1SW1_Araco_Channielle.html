Mili

Mili is a Japanese Indie music group founded in August 2012. It was established by Cassie Wei, a canadian vocalist, and a Japanese composer, Yamato Kasai. They were later joined by bassist Yukihito Mitomo, drummer Shoto Yoshida, and Illustrator Ao Fujimori. They have contributed music to various video games, animes and commericals. ITZY is a Kpop girl group under JYP Entertainment, as it consists of 5 members: Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna. They debuted on Feb. 12 2019, with their single album IT'z Different.

Blackpink

Blackpink is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment around August 8, 2016. It consists of 4 members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rose, and Lisa.
